# Design System — trafficcontrolinsurance.com

## Theme: High-Visibility DOT Authority (LIGHT / corporate)
Bright, clean, white-forward. Safety-industry visual language.

## Colors
- Primary / Safety Navy: #1A3A5C (authority, DOT professionalism)
- Accent / Hi-Vis Orange: #E67E22 (cones, vests, flagging)
- Signal Yellow: #F1C40F (sparing — warning chevrons, highlights)
- Background White: #FFFFFF
- Light Safety Gray: #F8F9FA (alternating sections)
- Text Dark: #212529 / muted #5A6470

## Typography
- Headings: Oswald (condensed, road-sign authority)
- Body: Open Sans

## Motifs
- Subtle diagonal chevron / road-marking stripe accents (navy/orange)
- Cone & flag iconography (lucide-react)
- DOT-style trust badges

## Components
- Sticky white navbar w/ navy logo + orange CTA button
- Cards: white, soft shadow, orange top-border on hover
- Buttons: orange primary, navy outline secondary
- motion/react reveal-on-scroll, lenis smooth scroll
